circle gets the square

about

How difficult is it to pay attention to little things? Let's explore the spaces of Arts Center.

This project was created by Amina Magomedova, a first-year student at NYUAD, for the 30MFF.

movie

“Circle gets the square” is a short movie about a simple thing - circles. Rooms are filled with shapes, but their repetitiveness goes unnoticed. A circle is a symbol of unity and perfection - visual harmony of the Arts Center is supported by it. This movie captures an unexpected occurrence of the ultimate geometric form in the interior space. As the saying goes, the circle gets the square - the idea is not complex, but it provokes the viewer to look around and pay attention to little things.
